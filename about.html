About Us

Ending Image-Based Abuse, or EIBA is a dedicated platform aimed at raising awareness about the harmful effects of image-based abuse and providing resources for individuals to protect themselves and others. Our mission is to inform, educate, and offer support to those affected by the non-consensual sharing of intimate images, commonly referred to as "revenge porn" or image-based abuse. We are committed to creating a safer digital environment through legal guidance, preventive measures, and community support.

EIBA provides resources open for victims of image based abuse to access. This will be to give them information and advice on what to do, the laws in their area, content removal requests, support services, and more. We hope that you can find use out of our webpage whether you're a victim, a friend, a student, a teacher, or a parent. Love, EIBA
