WHAT IS IMAGE BASED ABUSE?

The act of purposefully sharing or threatening to share an intimate image or video of someone without their consent for 'revenge' or manipulation. Common examples include someone doing it to hurt a person who has ended a relationship with them, or threatening to do it unless they stay together. Image based abuse is extremely common amongst young Australians such as yourselves, so it is important that all young people are educated on the risks, laws, and signs of image based abuse.
